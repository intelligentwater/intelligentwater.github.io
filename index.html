Intelligent Water

IWSD hosts and facilitates eight associated sectors in a construction biased business environment. It is a diverse portfolio that is intrinsically linked by our passion to projects and people.

It demonstrates the specialities that have been over the 40 years in the making. Its bedrock is our experience in building services and also the emerging new technologies and innovation required for the challenges we all face from climate change.

Our people make it happen. Enthusiastic, creative and adaptable, our associates strive for the very best and are alert for any new opportunities to provide our clients with a first class reliable and professional service.

Sustainable Sytems

Sustainable Sytems is our nuts and bolts services.

The uptake in sustainable energy systems received a big boost from the government with grant schemes and relaxations of planning rules to permit and encourage uptake. However, too many of the responses so far are far from acceptable.

IWSD's proposition is a more considered approach to ensure the PV panels and Heat Pumps are more sympathetically incorporated into your home or building. We take time and great care to optimise your investment and to ensure the systems are integrated into their surrounding.

A+R Consultancy

Advice and Resolution (A+R) is our specialist expert witness service established to support clients when the unexpected arises in their buildings assets.

A+R brings together the expertise from all of our sectors to identify and resolve the adversity affecting your business or investments. We have a wealth of experience at undertaking system forensic surveys, reporting and ensuring the situation is expertly concluded.

TBC Training

TBC training is a knowledge based organisation that promotes training and learning opportunities. These programmes can be delivered in-house, on-site or through self study on-line content. Programmes can delivered in traditional multi media forms or from its growing video library.

TBC training origins have evolved in the residential and commercial construction sectors and via personal development programmes for graduates and mature professionals in consulting engineering and its associated creative industries.

Video Production

Finally, we can reveal our newest service to the industry. This portfolio has been evolving over the years as an interest and a belief that developments in media technology can change the way information and training is delivered. A spin off from our training portfolio, we are able to offer high quality copyright and narratives for associated industries to promote their business in a fast growing and popular media format.

Ideas and Innovation
